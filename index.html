About plants

What is needed for a good mood?

Citrus can bloom and bear fruit in room conditions. A beautifully blooming and self-pollinating citrus tree will give you a good mood and juicy, tasty fruits. Trees in pots and containers are a great choice for those who don't have space for a garden. Or who wants to grow citrus trees on balconies, patios or indoors.




Lemon tree or kumquat, tangerine or grapefruit - all of them will please the eye, create a good mood and relax during rest. But for this to really happen, at some point the plant needs to be transplanted, fed and watered. It will also be necessary to keep at home in a heated room above 12 degrees in winter, shade from the active sun in summer.
